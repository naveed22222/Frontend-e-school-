Struggle Today Strength Tommorrow


The purpose of Eschool is to prepare students with the promise of enhancing their intellectual, physical, social, emotional, spiritual, and artistic growth so that they may realize their power for good as citizens of Pakistan.


Eschool aims at providing students with a well-rounded education offering:


  • A caring environment with a strong academic foundation
  • An exclusive focus on the individual child
  • A balance between local and global standards with modern approaches
  • Individual attention to students
